Spotlight Tournament Rules

These rules concern the Spotlight Tournament (hereby known as “spotlight”) for Vainglory. Failure to abide by these rules once entered may result in disqualification and banning from upcoming official Spotlight Tournaments.

Teams

  • Team members must be in the same team in-game.
  • Teams with offensive in-game names, handles or names may face penalties under organiser’s discretion.
  • Teams can only be entered by the captain of the team.
  • Teams must consist of at least 3 members, and can have a maximum of 6.
  • Team members must play in their original accounts, can’t share accounts with other individuals and cannot use smurfs as replacements.
  • No changes are allowed to the roster once the team has entered.
  • The organisers of the Spotlight reserve the power to refuse entry to a team without providing a reason.

Game Day

  • Teams must gather 5 minutes before the game and must have followed their allocated organiser and the streamer (if any) before this.
  • Failure to have a 3-man team before the start time will mean that the team will have another 5 minutes to have a full team or face penalties and forfeit the match.
  • Lag and other issues are the responsibility of the individual and won’t be taken into consideration.
  • Cheating, foul play or any inappropriate behaviour will result in penalties and a forfeit.
  • All players playing must connect to the Discord channel (provided via email or other correspondence) for their team to ensure no cheating is taking place.
  • A screenshot of the results (end screen where kills and deaths are shown) must be taken by captains of the two screens and sent to the organisers to verify results.
  • Any issues must be discussed and sorted 2 hours before the start of play. This includes issues such as inability to get a full team to play.

Streams and Spectating

  • Games will only be streamed by endorsed streamers.
  • Games are not allowed to be recorded by players.
  • Organisers reserve the rights to not stream games.
